ALSUWEIDI was built on a simple conviction: that engineering excellence and human integrity are not competing values — they are inseparable.

Founded in Abu Dhabi in 1988, we began as a small but resolute practice committed to getting things right. That commitment — to rigour, to our clients, to the cities we help build — has guided every decision since.

Today, we are a multidisciplinary consultancy with over 250 professionals spanning architecture, structural and MEP engineering, infrastructure, interior design, project management, and supervision. Our work spans the UAE and the wider region — from data centres to hospitals, from residential communities to hospitality icons.

In 2026, we refreshed our identity. The logo is new. The purpose is not.

What Drives Us

Where competence meets integrity.

Technical Rigour

Every calculation is verified. Every specification is deliberate. We hold ourselves to the highest technical standard because our work supports lives and livelihoods.

Founder Accountability

This is a founder-led firm. That means decisions are made by people with skin in the game — not passed through layers of management where accountability gets diluted.

Integrated Delivery

Seven disciplines under one roof means fewer gaps, fewer surprises, and a team that speaks the same language from concept to handover.

Local Knowledge

38 years in Abu Dhabi means we understand the climate, the regulators, the supply chain, and the culture. That knowledge is not teachable — it is earned.

Sustainability by Practice

LEED, BREEAM, Estidama — we were among the first in the region to pursue green ratings. Sustainability is not a badge for us; it is embedded in how we design.

People Development

1,100+ training hours in 2025. We invest in our team because the best projects come from the best engineers — and the best engineers keep growing.
